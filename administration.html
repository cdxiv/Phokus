University Council

Role: The University Council is the highest decision-making body at GCTU.

  • Vice-Chancellor
  • Pro-Vice-Chancellor
  • Registrar
  • Deans of Faculties
  • Other key staff members

The University Council is responsible for setting policies, strategic direction, and overall governance of the university.

Vice-Chancellor

Role: The Vice-Chancellor is the chief executive officer of the university.

Responsibilities: Strategic planning, academic leadership, external relations, and ensuring the university's mission and vision are achieved.

Administrative Departments

The administrative departments at GCTU are responsible for the day-to-day operations of the university.

  • Academic Affairs
  • Human Resources
  • Student Affairs
  • Examinations and Records

Support Units

The support units at GCTU provide additional services to students and staff.

  • Directorate of Information Communication Technology
  • Directorate of General Administration and Operations
  • Professional Development Unit

International Partnerships

GCTU has partnerships with international institutions to enhance academic programs and research initiatives.

  • Coventry University (UK)
  • Anhalt University of Applied Sciences (Germany)
  • Ramaiah University of Applied Sciences (India)
