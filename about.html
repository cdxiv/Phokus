History

GCTU was established in November 2005 and granted accreditation by the National Accreditation Board (NAB) on March 30, 2006. It was officially inaugurated on August 15, 2006, and gained full public university status on August 13, 2020. Over the years, GCTU has evolved from a polytechnic to a full-fledged university, expanding its academic offerings and infrastructure to meet the growing demands of communication technology education.

Mission and Vision

Mission:

GCTU is committed to providing an educational experience of the highest quality, focusing on technology-oriented and industry-relevant programs in ICT and related areas.

Vision:

The university aims to be a globally recognized center of research and intellectual creativity, striving for excellence in teaching and technology.
